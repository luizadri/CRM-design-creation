FAQs

CRM software, or Customer Relationship Management software, is a tool used by businesses to manage interactions and relationships with both current and potential customers. It typically includes features for organizing customer data, tracking interactions, automating tasks, and analyzing customer interactions to improve sales, marketing, and customer service efforts.

Contact management: The ability to store and organize customer contact information, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other relevant details. Interaction tracking: Tools for logging and tracking interactions with customers across various channels, such as email, phone calls, social media, and in-person meetings.
